About

Award winning producer/engineer/songwriter Dave Chappelle jr, opened Dave's Studio

in 2001. He built studio with his 28 cats and doesn't plan on slowing down anytime soon.

Dave's live room features a few half stacks. One labeled "ENGL", clearly

for the guitars or something, what looks like a fender twin, some sort of "Mesa" cab + head for

his warwick bass and a drumset. There's a keyboard too, but nobody likes keyboards. Moving on.

Dave's studio room features way too many monitors, a whole bunch of gear and

wires, a chair (FOR DAVE ONLY) and a lava lamp + dying plant for ambiance.
